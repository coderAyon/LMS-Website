Frequently Asked Questions

Have queries regarding catalog credentials or local cached buffering? We are here to assist.

Access is simple. Click the "Login / Register" button in the top-right corner. Use the Administrator account (admin@gmail.com with password admin1234) for system control, or register your own account to manage your shelf and access cached volumes.
The cache system uses your browser's persistent local storage. When you click "Buffer Offline" on a volume, its metadata profiles and reading credentials are downloaded locally, enabling zero-network access to your student database reading room!
Navigate to the "Catalog" section. You can use the search bar to search by title or author. You can also filter using the dynamic sidebar by subject category (e.g. Technology, Science, Fiction), semester tracking (1st to 8th semester), and status availability (Available, Reserved, Out of Stock).
Log in as "System Admin", click the "Admin Panel" tab in the nav bar, and select "Add Volume". Fill in details such as title, author, category, ISBN, publisher, and copies. You can also click "Edit" on any item in the admin list to update fields dynamically in real-time.
Saving any book adds it to your "My Library" personal vault dashboard and the "Saved Books" tab. You can manage your saved books, filter them by category, or sort them by title and average rating directly from your dashboard.
Any book modifications made through the Admin console are saved directly to local storage and propagate instantly across all pages. This means any search results, coverflow picks, or dashboard statistics update without requiring a manual page refresh.
